I am currently a senior at Michigan State University studying Advertising and Media & Information with a concentration in Interactive and Social Media.

I have three passions: traveling, music, and the internet. I have lived everywhere from Santa Monica, CA, to Shanghai, China, and little old Commerce, MI. Between moves I have visited over 15 countries where I developed my love for music, seeing how unique and inspiring to a culture it can be. Over the years, the internet has been a great companion; keeping me in touch with the people I meet, having the music I love, and fueling my wanderlust!

I am an aspiring professional eager to work in the fields of advertising, social media, marketing, and public relations. Welcome to my website!
